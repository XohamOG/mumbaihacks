🧠 OUR REASONING PROCESS:

🔬 VERIFICATION METHODS USED:
• Source Credibility Check: Mixed - accurate statistics from unreliable overall source
• Image/Video Authentication: Image appears authentic but misleading context
• Timeline Verification: Recent post using current vaccine data

🎯 WHY WE REACHED THIS CONCLUSION:
1. The effectiveness statistics come from legitimate studies and are accurately reported
2. The autism claim contradicts overwhelming scientific evidence from multiple independent studies
3. This appears to be a "truth sandwich" - mixing true information with false claims to increase credibility

🤔 AREAS OF UNCERTAINTY:
• Some vaccine side effect data is still being collected for rare events
• Long-term effectiveness data continues to be studied
• Individual risk factors may vary by person

📊 CONFIDENCE BREAKDOWN:
• Evidence Quality: Strong for autism claim (definitive studies), Strong for effectiveness (clinical trials)
• Source Reliability: Low overall (mixed reliable data from unreliable source)
• Expert Consensus: Very Strong (overwhelming medical agreement)

• Using emotional language about "hidden" information
• Making unsupported causal connections (correlation vs causation fallacy)

🎭 MANIPULATION TACTICS DETECTED:
• Truth sandwiching (mixing facts with falsehoods)
• Appeal to fear (protecting children from "hidden dangers")
• False authority (claiming government conspiracy)
• Cherry-picking data (ignoring contradicting studies)

📱 SPREAD PATTERNS:

## Key Features of the Knowledge Agent:

1. **Structured Format**: Clear, scannable layout with visual hierarchy
2. **Detailed Reasoning**: Explains WHY conclusions were reached
3. **Educational Value**: Teaches users critical thinking skills
4. **Transparency**: Shows limitations and uncertainty clearly
5. **Actionable Guidance**: Provides specific next steps for different users
6. **Evidence-Based**: All conclusions backed by verifiable sources
7. **User-Friendly**: Complex analysis made accessible to general public
